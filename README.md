# Awesome ML Research & Engineering Interviews

> A curated collection of the best free resources for Machine Learning Research Scientist, Research Engineer, Applied Scientist, and ML Systems interviews.
>
> Built from papers, courses, books, engineering resources, and real-world interview experiences from companies such as DeepMind, Anthropic, OpenAI, Meta, Cohere, Mistral, Tesla AI, and leading startups.

## Why This List Exists

Most interview guides are optimized for passing interviews.

The best researchers optimize for understanding.

This repository focuses on first principles, canonical papers, practical engineering, and research depth.

Whether you're interviewing for:

* Research Scientist
* Research Engineer
* Applied Scientist
* ML Systems Engineer
* Foundation Models Engineer
* AI Infrastructure Engineer

This list aims to provide a complete preparation roadmap.
